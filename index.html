How to play

The grid hides four words — one each of every length shown in the pills (e.g. 3, 4, 5, 6 letters). You don't get the words, only their lengths.

Trace a word by dragging across neighbouring letters — up, down, left or right, never diagonally. The path can bend. On desktop you can also click letters one at a time, then press Check (or Enter).

Every letter tile belongs to exactly one word, so use each tile once. Solve all four to stop the clock. Tap a finished word to release it. Hint reveals one letter of a word's path.

Shortcuts: Enter check · Backspace undo · Esc clear · H hint · N new.
